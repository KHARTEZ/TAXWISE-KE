1. Income Tax Changes

The bill proposes changes in income tax brackets, increasing rates for higher income earners and introducing a new minimum tax for businesses operating at a loss for consecutive years.

2. Value Added Tax (VAT)

Several essential goods and services previously exempt or zero-rated may now attract VAT. The bill aims to broaden the VAT base to increase revenue.

3. Digital Services Tax

The bill expands the scope of Digital Services Tax to include influencers, content creators, and foreign platforms operating in Kenya, at a proposed rate of 5%.

4. Excise Duties

New excise duty rates are introduced for sugary drinks, plastic packaging, betting/gambling services, and imported second-hand clothing.

5. Other Notable Proposals

- A proposed eco-tax on electronics
- Mandatory KRA registration for mobile money agents
- Adjustments to turnover tax for SMEs
