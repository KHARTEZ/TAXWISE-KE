Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Finance Bill 2025?

The Finance Bill 2025 is a proposed law outlining how the government plans to raise revenue and manage public funds in the upcoming year.

Why does it matter to ordinary citizens?

The bill could introduce new taxes or changes that affect salaries, goods, services, and business operations — which impacts everyday life.

Where can I read the full bill?

You can access it through the Parliament of Kenya website or on our page under "The Bill 2025".

How does Taxwise KE help?

We break down the bill into simple language, provide visuals, expert analysis, and community discussions to help every Kenyan understand.
